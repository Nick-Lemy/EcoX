Recycling Tips

Organic Waste

Compost organic waste to reduce methane emissions from landfills and create nutrient-rich soil for plants.

Plastic Recycling

Rinse plastic containers before recycling and check the recycling number on the bottom to ensure it's accepted by your local facility.

Paper Recycling

Remove any plastic windows or metal bindings from paper items before recycling. Shredded paper can often be composted instead of recycled.
